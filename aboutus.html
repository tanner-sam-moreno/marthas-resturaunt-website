About Us

Our Story

Martha's started as a small hole-in-the-wall restaurant with a simple goal: serve authentic Mexican food made with the same care and tradition found in family kitchens. Martha, the sister of the brothers who founded La Esperanza in Cutler, brought her own passion and family recipes to Orosi.

As more and more families discovered the restaurant, its popularity quickly grew. What began as a modest location soon became a local favorite, leading Martha to move the restaurant to its current home so she could continue sharing her food with an even larger community.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to provide authentic Mexican cuisine in a warm and welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home. We believe great food brings people together, which is why we are proud to offer a family-oriented environment built on tradition, hospitality, and community.

Meet the Owner

Martha has spent years perfecting the recipes and techniques passed down through her family. Inspired by her culinary roots and a lifelong love of cooking, she takes pride in preparing dishes that honor authentic Mexican flavors while creating memorable experiences for every guest who walks through the door.

What Makes Us Special

From treasured family recipes to homemade dishes prepared fresh each day, Martha's is known for its authentic flavors and dedication to quality. Our strong connection to the community and commitment to making every guest feel like family are what truly set us apart.
